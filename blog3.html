Mahindra BE 6e & XEV 9e: Features, Price & Launch in India

Mahindra has officially entered the premium electric SUV market with the launch of the BE 6e and XEV 9e models. Priced at ₹18.9 lakh and ₹21.9 lakh respectively, these SUVs are built to redefine sustainable transportation in India. With their sleek design, impressive range, and advanced features, Mahindra’s offerings challenge competitors in both the EV and ICE (internal combustion engine) segments.

Key Features of BE 6e & XEV 9e

Both the BE 6e and XEV 9e come equipped with cutting-edge technology, offering high efficiency and driver-centric features. Here are some standout features:

  • Range: BE 6e delivers 682 km, while the XEV 9e offers 656 km, both certified by ARAI.
  • Performance: Both SUVs accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 6.2 seconds, thanks to their powerful electric motors.
  • Fast Charging: Equipped with a 175kW DC charger, they charge from 20% to 80% in just 20 minutes, making them practical for long journeys.
  • Safety: The XEV 9e includes seven airbags, ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems), and a 360-degree camera system.
  • Battery Technology: Powered by BYD's advanced lithium iron phosphate batteries, offering durability and safety.

Unique Design & Interior

The BE 6e and XEV 9e boast a futuristic design that blends aerodynamics with elegance. Key design highlights include:

  • Distinctive LED lighting, giving the SUVs a premium and modern look.
  • Panoramic sunroof for an enhanced in-cabin experience.
  • Spacious interiors with vegan leather upholstery, underlining Mahindra’s commitment to sustainability.

The dashboard features dual 12.3-inch touchscreens with connected car technology, providing seamless navigation, entertainment, and vehicle monitoring.

Pricing & Competitive Analysis

Mahindra’s aggressive pricing strategy is designed to disrupt the Indian EV market. Here’s how they stack up:

Model Price Range (ARAI) Charging Time
Mahindra BE 6e ₹18.9 lakh 682 km 20 minutes (fast charging)
Mahindra XEV 9e ₹21.9 lakh 656 km 20 minutes (fast charging)
Hyundai Creta EV ₹23 lakh 500 km 25 minutes (fast charging)

Compared to rivals like the Hyundai Creta EV, Mahindra offers better range, safety, and affordability, making it an attractive option for buyers transitioning to EVs.

Mahindra’s EV Vision: A Green Future

Mahindra is investing ₹16,000 crore in its EV venture, aiming to produce 10,000 electric SUVs annually by March 2025. By leveraging the INGLO platform, Mahindra ensures optimal efficiency, safety, and scalability for its EVs. This initiative is part of the company's broader strategy to achieve carbon neutrality and become a leader in sustainable transportation.

In addition to SUVs, Mahindra is exploring partnerships with charging infrastructure providers to make EV adoption seamless for Indian consumers.

Why Choose the BE 6e or XEV 9e?

Here’s why Mahindra’s latest SUVs are worth considering:

  • Long Range: Ideal for city commutes and long drives.
  • Cost-Effective: Priced competitively to offer excellent value.
  • Eco-Friendly: Built with a focus on sustainability and carbon neutrality.
  • Safety First: Advanced safety features to ensure peace of mind.

Conclusion

With the launch of the BE 6e and XEV 9e, Mahindra has proven its commitment to a sustainable and electric future. These SUVs redefine performance, affordability, and safety in the Indian EV market, making them a compelling choice for buyers. If you’re planning to switch to an electric vehicle, Mahindra’s latest offerings are worth a close look.
